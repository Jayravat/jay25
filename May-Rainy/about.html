About Us

In 1980, we started RAVAT FURNITURE with a vision - to make a million homes beautiful. Back then, our catalogue featured just 35 designs. Yet, when our customers shared photographs of their homes, we saw they were remarkably distinct. The same table, the same chair was used differently by different people. This told us something. People want their homes to be unique.

Ravat Carpentry

RAVAT CARPENTRY Co.,Ltd. established in 1971, is a professional wholesale supplier of furniture, with design, R&D, production, and sales, offering OEM and ODM services. Our core product is solid wood furniture, living room furniture, dining furniture, office furniture, outdoor furniture, and so on.

We stepped into the furniture business for more than 25 years. As a professional we uphold the highest quality standards and integrity in our furniture design. Our mission is to offer the best raw materials, design, and technique for each furniture piece, making our product not only stylish but also highly durable Carpentry may be defined as the process of making wooden goods and components, viz. roofs, floors, partitions, doors, etc.

Skilled carpenter

Carpenters are skilled artisans who construct, install and renovate structures made of wood and other materials, ranging from kitchen cabinets to building frameworks. They work in various areas of construction.
